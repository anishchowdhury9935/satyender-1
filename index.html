Patent Search

Our Patent Search Service offers comprehensive and precise searches to identify prior art and assess the novelty of your invention. Leveraging advanced databases like Espacenet, USPTO, and Google Patents, we provide detailed reports that help you navigate potential challenges and streamline the patent filing process. Trust our expertise to ensure your innovation is protected with confidence.

Patent Analytics

Our Patent Analytics Service delivers in-depth insights into patent landscapes, helping you identify trends, competitive activity, and potential opportunities in your field. Using advanced tools and techniques, we analyze patent data to provide strategic guidance for your R&D and business decisions. Empower your innovation strategy with data-driven patent intelligence.

Patent and Design Filing

Our Patent and Design Filing Service ensures that your intellectual property is meticulously protected, from initial concept to final submission. We handle the entire process, including drafting, preparing, and filing applications for patents and designs, adhering to all legal requirements. With our expertise, secure your innovations and creative designs with confidence and ease.

Trademark Service

Our Trademark Service is dedicated to safeguarding your brand identity by offering comprehensive trademark search, registration, and enforcement support. We assist you in every step, from selecting a distinctive mark to navigating the legal intricacies of registration. Protect your brand's unique value and prevent infringement with our expert trademark solutions.

Business Intelligence

Our Business Intelligence service transforms raw data into actionable insights, empowering you to make informed strategic decisions. We analyze market trends, competitive landscapes, and customer behavior to provide a clear picture of your business environment. With our expertise, stay ahead of the competition and drive growth through data-driven strategies.

Licensing & Litigation

Our Licensing & Litigation service offers expert guidance on navigating the complexities of intellectual property rights. We assist in negotiating and drafting licensing agreements to maximize the value of your IP assets while ensuring compliance with legal standards. In case of disputes, our litigation support provides robust defense or enforcement of your rights, helping you protect and monetize your intellectual property effectively.
